template<typename TInput, typename TLevelSetContainer>
class itk::LevelSetEquationBinaryMaskTerm< TInput, TLevelSetContainer >

Class to represent the mask boundary as a negative energy.

\[ M\left( p \right) \cdot \]

  • \( M \) is a mask with 0 representing a boundary,
